How Does 0% US Tax Work?

If you meet the following conditions, you may qualify for 0% US tax:

  • You are not a US citizen or resident
  • You do not have employees in the United States
  • You do not receive payments from US clients

Instead, you pay taxes only in the country where you reside.

Frequently Asked Questions.

Everything you need to know about opening a US LLC as a non-resident.

Do I Need to Travel to the USA?

No. The entire process is done online. We communicate with the IRS and other relevant authorities on your behalf.

You simply provide the required information, and within 20-30 business days your company is fully ready to operate.

What Are the Ongoing Costs of a US LLC?

US LLC obligations are handled on an annual basis. In addition to the IRS, there are state-level fees such as the registered agent and state maintenance.

Each state has different requirements, but we typically recommend Wyoming due to its low annual cost (around $600-$800 total).

Unlike local business structures, there is no requirement for monthly accounting or a minimum tax threshold, allowing you to fully focus on growing your business.

What Are the Requirements for Qualifying for 0% US Tax?

By forming a Non-Resident LLC, non-resident entrepreneurs may operate without US tax obligations under certain conditions. To qualify:

  • You are not a US citizen or resident
  • You have no employees in the United States
  • You do not receive payments from US clients
How Long Does It Take to Open a US Company?

Here's the typical timeline after you submit your application:

  • 24-48 hours - LLC registration is filed and confirmed
  • 2-3 weeks - EIN (Employer Identification Number) issued by the IRS
  • ~3 days - Bank accounts and payment processors (Stripe, PayPal, etc.) ready to use
Where Can I Charge for My Services?

You can charge for your services globally, in any country including your country of residence. Everything is fully legal.

Can I Invoice Multiple Services Through One US Company?

Yes. You can invoice multiple services through a single company, as long as none of them require special licenses.

Can I Have Multiple Bank Accounts?

Yes. Just like in other jurisdictions, it is possible to have multiple bank accounts under one LLC.

Do I Need a US Tax Advisor When Opening an LLC?

For basic company setup, a tax advisor is not required.

However, for annual reports and tax filings, working with professionals is recommended. We provide access to certified US-based partners who can assist non-resident LLC clients.

How Can I Protect My Personal Privacy?

By registering your LLC through our service, you receive a registered agent with a business address. This helps protect your personal address from being publicly visible in official records.

How Quickly Can I Start Receiving Payments?

In most cases, you can start receiving payments within 7-14 days after your LLC is formed and your bank account is activated.

Can I Open an LLC If I Already Have a Company in Another Country?

Yes. It is very common for entrepreneurs to own a local company and also open a US LLC.

The LLC can be used for international operations, payments in foreign currencies, and integration with global payment systems.

Can LLC Ownership Be Transferred to Another Person?

Yes. Ownership can be transferred or sold, but it must be properly documented through an operating agreement and in accordance with the laws of the state where the LLC is registered.
