1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you register, purchase, or otherwise use our Services, we collect personally identifiable information ("Personal Information") that you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Full name and contact details (email address, phone number, mailing address)
  • Government-issued identification information required for U.S. company formation (e.g., passport, national ID)
  • Business information (company name, desired state of formation, business type)
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party payment processors - we do not store full card details)
  • Communications you send us via email, chat, or support tickets
  • Any other information you choose to provide when using the Services

Please note: if you choose not to provide certain Personal Information, we may be unable to provide certain parts of our Services.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you access or use our Services, we automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Device type, operating system, and browser type
  • Pages visited, links clicked, and time spent on pages
  • Referring website address
  • Access dates and times
  • Other diagnostic and usage data

We collect this information using technologies such as cookies, web beacons, pixel tags, server logs, and similar tracking technologies.

1.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies; however, some parts of our Services may not function properly if you do so. We may also use third-party services such as Google Analytics that use their own cookies.

1.4 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from third parties, such as government agencies (in the context of company formation filings), banking partners, and registered agent providers, where such sharing is necessary to provide the Services.

  • Process and fulfill your company formation order
  • Apply for your EIN with the IRS on your behalf

  • Send order confirmations, status updates, and important notices

  • Send marketing and promotional communications (where you have consented; you may opt out at any time)

2.3 To Improve and Protect Our Services

  • Analyze usage trends and monitor performance
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activity
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

2.4 Legal Bases for Processing (EEA/UK Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, our legal bases for processing are: contractual necessity; legal obligation; legitimate interests (e.g., fraud prevention, service improvement); and consent where explicitly given, which you may withdraw at any time.

3. COMMUNICATION PREFERENCES - SMS, CALLS & TCPA NOTICE

TCPA CONSENT NOTICE: By providing your telephone number and opting in to SMS or voice communications, you expressly consent - under the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA), 47 U.S.C. § 227, and applicable state laws - to receive autodialed or pre-recorded calls and/or text messages from Otvori LLC / Formio.biz at the number provided. Consent is not a condition of purchase.

3.1 Types of Communications

If you provide your mobile phone number and opt in, we may contact you via SMS or voice call for the following purposes:

  • Order status updates and formation progress notifications
  • Account security alerts (e.g., one-time passcodes)
  • Customer support follow-up calls or messages
  • Promotional offers and service announcements (only where you have given separate explicit consent)

3.2 How to Opt In

You may opt in to SMS communications by: (a) checking the relevant consent checkbox on our website at the time of purchase or account registration; (b) texting START to our designated shortcode or long code as shown on our website; or (c) providing written consent to our support team.

3.3 How to Opt Out

You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any SMS message you receive from us - you will receive one final confirmation message and then no further SMS messages
  • Replying HELP for assistance or contact information
  • Contacting us at support@formio.biz with "SMS Opt-Out" in the subject line

Please allow up to 10 business days to process your opt-out request. Opting out of promotional SMS does not affect transactional or service-related communications that are required for your active order.

3.4 Message Frequency and Costs

Message frequency varies depending on your order status and account activity. Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan. Otvori LLC is not responsible for any charges incurred from your mobile carrier in connection with SMS communications.

3.5 Email Marketing Opt-Out

You may opt out of marketing emails at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any marketing email or by contacting us at support@formio.biz. You will continue to receive transactional and service-related emails related to your active order.
